APPROPRIATENESS EVALUATION CRITERIA

Language Complexity

Categorizes the complexity of the language used in the video in terms of grade-level categories (Kindergarten, Elementary School (Grades 1-5), Middle School (Grades 6-8), High School (Grades 9-12))

Presentation Complexity

Categorizes the complexity of the video presentation style into two categories (Simple, Hard) based on the amount of text, graphics, and animations, and the organization of information
